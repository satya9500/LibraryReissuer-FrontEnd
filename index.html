LIBRARY REISSUER

An application to reissue the books which have reached its due date.

  • Auto-Reissue: Reissues book if it has reached its due date.
  • Reminder: If only one day is left then you get a reminder to reissue/return your book immediately.
  • Mailer: A mail is sent if the books are reissued automaticaly or they can't be ressued online anymore.
